About Us

Faith, devotion and community togetherness

Mithapali Kirttan Mandali is a devotional group organised by Mithapali Gramabasi Brunda. Our aim is to spread spiritual values, bhakti, peace and cultural heritage through naam sankirtan, bhajans and religious gatherings.

This website is made to showcase our events, memories, photo gallery, video gallery and contact details, so everyone can stay connected with our mandali activities.
